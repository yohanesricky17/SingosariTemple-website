Background

Singosari Temple is a Hindu temple from the historical heritage of the Singasari Kingdom located in Candirenggo Village, Singosari District, Malang Regency, East Java, Indonesia, about 10 km from Malang City. We created this website and application in order to fulfill the assignments of the Human Computer Interaction course, as well as in order to increase public awareness of the importance of appreciating and preserving the historical heritage that is around us. We hope this website could gain younger generation's desire to learn about history. The younger generations are our last hope to save and preserve things that belong to our nation and our country...
